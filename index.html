Help Us Open Madrona
Laise Bien is a non-profit project (ASBL) to open a lesbian and feminist community space in Brussels. Imagine: coffee during the day, drinks later on, feminist books everywhere, good conversations, soft lighting, and zero tolerance for nonsense.

We have found a space. It is… incredibly empty. As in: no toilets. As in: “the electricity does whatever it wants”. So before we can welcome you in, we need to make the place legally, safely, and humanly usable.

What the money is for
To open Madrona, we need to fund the not-so-glamorous essentials:
- Installing toilets (because society insists)
- Fixing and bringing the electricity up to code
- Setting up internet and basic infrastructure
- Making the space safe and compliant for public access

The goal
We have until june to raise €45,000. This money goes directly toward turning an empty shell into a real, functional, and welcoming space.

Why MADRONA
Lesbian spaces are rare. Laise Bien ASBL is out to change that. A place where you can:
- Come alone or with friends
- Read feminist and lesbian books
- Have a coffee, then a drink, then maybe another
- Exist without having to explain yourself
- Feel safe, relaxed, and at home

It’s not just a bar. It’s a living room for the community.
